Abide Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 19, 2026

What We Store

Abide stores devotion progress, settings, and saved devotions locally in your browser on your device.

Google Drive Sync

If you sign in with Google, Abide may store a single JSON file in your Google Drive app-data folder to sync saved devotions between your devices. Abide requests only the minimum Drive app-data scope.

Data Sharing

Abide does not sell personal data. External APIs are used only to provide devotional generation and scripture functionality.

Contact

For support, contact the app developer.
